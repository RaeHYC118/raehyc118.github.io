Teaching & Outreach

Brain-Computer Interface Science Camp

Designed and organized a hands-on BCI workshop at Academia Sinica 〔年份〕. I built the curriculum, wrote the SSVEP analysis pipeline participants work through, and adapted our interns' assistive-robot project into a teaching platform they could drive with their own EEG. During the hands-on sessions I worked alongside the participants, helping them through the analysis and talking with them about the field.
